About Us

In today's fast-evolving technological landscape, it is imperative to equip the younger generation with essential skills to navigate the complexities of the digital age. Recognizing this need, Reboot Robotics Academy, a Kolkata, West Bengal-based MSME, an esteemed institution, registered under the Government of India and complies with ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System standards. Additionally, it holds accreditation from the International Accreditation Forum (IAF) presents a comprehensive Technical Training program & Robotics Lab SetUp Initiative designed to introduce students from Schools, Colleges & Upskilling Professionals to the fascinating world of Robotics, S.T.E.M (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), and Artificial Intelligence (AI). As an official Institute Member of the All India Council for Robotics & Automation (AICRA), Reboot Robotics Academy is dedicated to providing comprehensive technical education. Our primary focus lies in providing In-school technical training, industrial training for college students, and vocational courses to professionals in the fields of Robotics, Coding, and AI. This aligns with the National Education Policy of 2020.

Robotics StartUp of the Year

OCTOBER, 2023

Reboot Robotics Academy proudly received the Best Woman Educational Entrepreneurship of the Year Award at the India S.T.E.M Summit 2023, presented by AICRA. Under the leadership of Smt. Anshu Dubey, the academy was recognized for its remarkable contributions to STEM education and innovation. The award was presented in October 2023, in the presence of esteemed dignitaries, including Shri VK Saraswat (Niti Aayog), Prof. Mamidala Jagadesh Kumar (UGC Chairman), and Shri T G Sitharam (AICTE Chairman). This achievement reinforces Reboot Robotics Academy’s commitment to empowering future innovators.
